The Premise Of Gigster:

Freelance musicians consistently find that one of the hardest things to do is maintain all the repertoire they are required to know. With that in mind Gigster set out to make an app that would help all gigging instrumentalists easily organize all the artists sets and studio song lists that they need to keep track of. Utilizing the great power of React.Js Gigster provides an intuitive application that is sure to help all overwhelmed musicians keep all their gigs!
